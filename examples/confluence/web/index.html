How to Play

Confluence is a real-time territory game where 2-4 players compete to control the most cells on a 10×10 grid.

Objective

Control the most territory when the 30-second timer runs out. You control cells by placing tokens adjacent to them.

Actions

  • Place: Click an empty cell to place a strength-1 token
  • Merge: Click your token, then an adjacent token to combine them (max strength 3)
  • Split: Click a strength-2+ token, then an adjacent empty cell to split it

Scoring

  • Cells with only your tokens = controlled (1 point)
  • Cells with tokens from multiple players = contested (no points, but blocks opponents)
  • Empty cells adjacent to only your tokens = influence (1 point)
CRDT Magic: When two players place on the same cell simultaneously, BOTH tokens are preserved as a contested cell. No last-write-wins — the CRDT merges concurrent edits!

Pro Tips

  • Higher strength tokens don't score more, but they can split to expand faster
  • Contested cells block your opponent from controlling that area
  • Watch the provenance: merged tokens show their parent history on hover
