# Space Actions

Operations on the game space and zones. Place, move, arrange tokens in spatial layouts.

[← Back to Action Reference](../ACTIONS.md)

---

## Actions (13)

**Basic Operations**
1. `space:place` - Place token in zone
2. `space:clear` - Clear entire space
3. `space:move` - Move token between zones
4. `space:flip` - Flip token face up/down
5. `space:remove` - Remove token from space

**Zone Management**
6. `space:createZone` - Create new zone
7. `space:deleteZone` - Delete zone
8. `space:clearZone` - Clear specific zone

**Zone Operations**
9. `space:shuffleZone` - Randomize positions in zone
10. `space:transferZone` - Move all tokens between zones
11. `space:fanZone` - Arrange in fan layout
12. `space:stackZone` - Stack tokens vertically
13. `space:spreadZone` - Spread in a line
14. `space:lockZone` - Lock/unlock zone
